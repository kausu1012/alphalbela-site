Significance of Crops

Crops are the backbone of rural life, providing food, income, and cultural identity. Understanding their importance helps us appreciate our roots.

Featured Crops

  • Wheat

    Wheat is a staple food crop that plays a crucial role in the diet of many communities.

  • Rice

    Rice is a primary source of food for more than half of the world's population, especially in Asia.

  • Maize

    Maize is versatile and used for food, fodder, and industrial products.
